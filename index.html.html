Quick Tips

Make life easier

  • Set 3 phone alarms: 08:00, 14:00, 22:00. Label them.
  • Always take the morning meds after breakfast — food protects your stomach.
  • Never skip Prednisolone or stop suddenly — it must taper down properly.
  • If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember unless it's nearly time for the next one. Don't double up.
